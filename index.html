Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2024

Welcome to Japanese Walking Trainer ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Our app collects the following types of information:

  • Physical Activity Data: We request access to your device's physical activity data (via ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION permission) to monitor your walking status during a workout. This data is processed on your device in real-time to guide you through the workout intervals and is not stored or shared.
  • Device Information: We collect basic device information such as device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics purposes.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app's performance and user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of the interval walking workout.
  • To improve our app's functionality and user experience.
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • To analyze app performance and fix technical issues.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob's privacy policy can be found at https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543.

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Your physical activity data is processed ephemerally on your device for the duration of a workout and is not stored or transmitted off the device. Anonymous usage data may be stored to help us improve the app.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information that we hold.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt-out of personalized advertisements.
  • Control physical activity permissions through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
